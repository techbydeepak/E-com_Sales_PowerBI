# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard 📊

## Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze online sales performance and business insights. This dashboard helps track sales amount, profit, quantity sold, customer purchasing behavior, payment modes, and product category performance through interactive visualizations.

The project focuses on identifying sales trends, profitable product categories, customer patterns, and state-wise sales distribution to support better business decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Track profit trends and monthly performance
* Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Analyze payment mode preferences
* Explore state-wise sales distribution

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* **Power BI** – Dashboard Development
* **CSV Dataset** – Data Source
* **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation
* **DAX** – KPI Calculations

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Metrics

* **Total Sales Amount:** 438K
* **Total Profit:** 37K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 5615
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 121K

### Key Insights

* **Clothing category** contributes the highest sales quantity.
* **COD (Cash on Delivery)** is the most preferred payment method.
* **Printers** generate the highest profit among sub-categories.
* Monthly profit trends remain relatively stable throughout the year.
* Multiple states contribute significantly to overall sales performance.
